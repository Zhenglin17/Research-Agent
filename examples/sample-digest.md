Cell: A TCR-mimic platform targeting intracellular neoantigens via HLA
presentation
Introduces "NeoTCR", a yeast-display pipeline that generates soluble TCR mimics
against peptide-HLA complexes derived from intracellular proteins. Demonstrates
in-vivo tumor regression in two HLA-humanized mouse models. Key claim: extends
the neoantigen-targetable space beyond the ~10% addressable by surface-antigen
therapies. Industrial collaboration with Adaptive Biotechnologies disclosed.
https://www.cell.com/cell/example-neotcr

bioRxiv: Spatial single-cell atlas of exhausted T cells in colorectal cancer
Maps 1.2M cells across 87 CRC samples and identifies three functionally
distinct exhausted CD8+ T-cell states, only one of which correlates with
response to anti-PD-1. Provides a candidate biomarker panel (Tex-3 signature)
for patient stratification. Still a preprint; clinical validation pending.
https://www.biorxiv.org/content/example-tex-atlas
